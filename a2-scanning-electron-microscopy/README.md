# A2 – Scanning Electron Microscopy

The experiment used scanning electron microscopy to investigate the structural, compositional, and geometric properties of a semiconductor sample, including calibration-based size measurements, qualitative EDX analysis, and determination of a layer thickness.

## Focus
- SEM imaging of semiconductor structures
- secondary-electron and backscattered-electron contrast
- calibration and dimensional measurements
- qualitative chemical analysis using EDX
- layer-thickness determination from cross-sectional imaging
