# O09e – Fringes of Equal Thickness

The experiment used optical interference to investigate Newton’s rings and wedge interference, including the determination of the radius of curvature of a lens and the thickness of aluminium foil and a strand of hair.

## Focus
- Newton’s rings
- wedge interference
- radius of curvature measurement
- thickness measurement of thin objects using optical interference
