# E13 – Switching Phenomena

The experiment investigated the voltage response of a capacitor in a MOSFET-switched circuit, using an RC model for charging and an underdamped RLC-type model to study the discharge behavior and parasitic switching effects.

## Focus
- capacitor charging and discharging in a switched circuit
- RC charging behavior
- oscillatory discharge response
- modeling of transient switching dynamics
- parasitic effects in high-speed switching
