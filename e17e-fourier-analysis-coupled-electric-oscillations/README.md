# E17e – Fourier Analysis of Coupled Electric Oscillations

The experiment studied coupled LC oscillators under capacitive and inductive coupling, including the analysis of frequency splitting, coupling factors, beat oscillations, and the distance dependence of mutual inductance.

## Focus
- capacitive and inductive coupling
- low-point and high-point circuit configurations
- resonance frequencies and coupling strength
- beat oscillations and mutual inductance analysis

- `report.pdf` – final lab report
