# O12 – Atomic Spectra

The experiment used a reflective diffraction grating to determine the grating constant, calculate wavelengths of sodium spectral doublets, and estimate the resolving power of the grating.

## Focus
- mercury and sodium emission spectra
- diffraction grating constant
- wavelength determination from angular measurements
- resolving power of a diffraction grating
